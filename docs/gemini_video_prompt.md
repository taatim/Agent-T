# Prompt for Google Gemini Video Generator: Agent T Whiteboard Explainer

**Goal:** Create a 60-second animated "whiteboard explainer" video for YouTube about "Agent T".
**Style:** Hand-drawn doodle aesthetic on a white background. Fast-paced, engaging, red and blue marker colors for emphasis.
**Voiceover:** Energetic, professional narrator.

---
**Input Script & Visual Plan:**

**Scene 1: The Problem (0:00-0:10)**
*   **Visual:** Draw a stick figure holding a phone, looking frustrated. Draw a robot icon with a red "X" over it.
*   **Narration:** "Ever called customer support and got stuck talking to a dumb robot? Or worse, an AI that booked your appointment three months late?"

**Scene 2: Introducing Agent T (0:10-0:20)**
*   **Visual:** Erase the robot. Draw the "Agent T" logo (a shield with a T). Write text: "Agent T: Human-in-the-Loop".
*   **Narration:** "Meet Agent T. The open-source voice agent that restores human agency."

**Scene 3: How It Works (0:20-0:40)**
*   **Visual:** Draw the flow diagram from left to right:
    1.  Caller -> (Phone Icon) Azure Cloud.
    2.  Cloud -> (Ear Icon) AI Speech.

    4.  Human stick figure typing on the laptop.
    5.  Laptop -> (Mouth Icon) "Hello!".
*   **Narration:** "It uses Azure AI to transcribe calls instantly. But here's the magic: It streams the text to a live human operator who types the response. The AI speaks it back in real-time."

**Scene 4: The Mission (0:40-0:50)**
*   **Visual:** Draw a boxing glove punching a robot. Text: "Good AI vs Bad AI".
*   **Narration:** "It's time to fight bad AI with good AI. Agent T gives you superpowers to negotiate appointments and navigate complex phone trees."

**Scene 5: Call to Action (0:50-1:00)**
